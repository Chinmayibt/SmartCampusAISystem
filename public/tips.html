Study habits

  • Time-box — Match the suggested slot length in Settings to how long you can actually focus.
  • One next step — For each task, write the smallest next action in the notes field.
  • Review the dashboard — A quick morning glance at reminders beats cramming the night before.
  • Log minutes honestly — Even 15 minutes counts; it builds a realistic picture on Progress.
  • Protect sleep — Keep your “latest study hour” in Settings aligned with when you actually stop.
  • Use Focus mode — Turn off other tabs for one Pomodoro, then take the short break.
